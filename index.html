About

As a high school student who is now going to college, I have learned that scheduling, making a to-do list, and accomplishing them is crucial to finishing my work on time.
However, planners and to-do list webpages/apps currently on the market do not have the functions I want and are uncomfortable to use. Therefore, I decided to design my own todo-list for myself and other students.




First Page : Monthly Calendar Page

You can choose the desired month with the button above and fill in the boxes showing the date with events or work to do on that day.




Second Page : To-do List Page

Shows a clear and editable daily to-do list, that shows how many to do things you have planned for each day. This can either be shown as completed, imcompleted, and can be editted or deleted.




Third Page : Timer Page

You can choose what background image to show up. There is also a timer that you could set a certain time limit that you could use to study.
